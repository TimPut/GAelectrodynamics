\makedefinition{Reverse}{dfn:reverse:1}{

The reverse \( A^\dagger \) of a grade-k blade \( A = \Ba_1 \Ba_2 \cdots \Ba_k \) is

\begin{equation*}
A^\dagger = \Ba_k \Ba_{k-1} \cdots \Ba_1.
\end{equation*}

The reverse of a multivector is the sum of the reversions of all its constituient grades.
} % definition
